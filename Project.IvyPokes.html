Useful Information

What is Handpoke? ▿

Handpoke is a tattoo technique. It is not a style like many people think. Handpoke tattoo is done without a machine meaning that the artist is using only a needle and ink and every dot is made by hand. All the materials that are used during the session are sterile and used for one client only.

Pro's and Con's of Handpoke ▿

Handpoke tattoos are less invasive for the skin which makes them heal faster. It is said that such tattoos hurt not a much as machine ones. However, this strongly depends on the person's pain threshold. Handpoke is a delicate technique and is ideal for finger and ear tattoos. One of the cons of handpoked tattoos is time. It is time consuming and some projects can take up to several hours even if they are not big in size. That is why it is always important to provide the approximate size of your design so that we could estimate the efforts and time.

To Do Before the Session ▿

Before the session I recommend the following:
- to exclude caffeine, alcohol, any other blood thinners and medications that can increase blood pressure. Such substances can cause bleeding during the session and may affect the healing process.
- to get enough sleep and have a meal. Especially if you're gonna have a lengthy session. This will keep you on track and you won't faint into a hungry swoon.
- to wear something comfy and casual that won't hinder your movements.
- to be in a good mood :)

Aftercare ▿

Healing process may take up to two weeks. The complete healing and skin regeneration happens in a month after the session. During the first two weeks please avoid:
- hot bath, swimming pool, sauna, solarium, direct sun exposure;
- steaming out your tattoo;
- scratching or ripping off the crust.
The final look of your tattoo very much depends on the care you provide to it.
